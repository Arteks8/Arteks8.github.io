Where am going?

My way is complex and complicated. I started to do music late; sometimes, it takes work to stay on track. Responsibilities, studying, work, and life difficulties make the way to my dream harder. But I still go. I want to feel, create, and perform my music, the music that touches my soul and thousands of others. And to be honest, the more I go, the more I realize that I am on the right track and one day, I will be where I want to be. I am grateful to people who love and support me. I believe in myself and in what I am doing. And it is hard sometimes, but it is totally ok, because:

"Music is the strongest form of magic" -Marlyn Manson

And learning magic is hard. But for me it is worth it!
