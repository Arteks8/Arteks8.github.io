My path

In the beginning

In the beginning, there was a guitar... My path in music started when I was 15 years old. I was never interested in playing instruments before, but when I started the guitar, I could not stop. I was playing day and night and learning every song I liked. I had hard times and good times, but I was still playing music because I loved it. I was playing my nice black classical guitar and even performed on my prompt. However, everything changed the day after my 17th birthday...

Electrification

A day after my 17th birthday, I went to a party at my friend's house. And guys brought it-electric guitar. I was super excited and played for a whole evening. I worked for approximately a month that summer and bought my first electric guitar. I fell in love with this instrument and started to explore the vast world of heavy music. Soon, I entered university and met a lot of creative people. In my first year, my friend and I performed our song at the university's talent show; I also played in different music events. All that time, I was mastering my skills and learning to play the instrument.

Diseased Convergence

When my second year just started, I met my good friend who also plays guitar. We wanted to play in a band but needed a drummer and bass player. After some time, the Lord sent us a fantastic bass player. A drummer just appeared from nowhere, and we created a band. We debated the name: a part of the band wanted "Convergence," and another: "Diseased." After a long time, we found a compromise: "Diseased Convergence" was chosen as a name. We practiced and performed our song on our university show shortly and won! It was my first band performance, which was a great success! I am grateful to the guys and happy to play with them. After the talent night, we were jamming together a lot. Unfortunately, we will not perform on the talent show this year. Still, I'm happy to play with the guys even without performing on a talent show. I feel my development, and I am going.
